Top 10 item picks for baby shopping.

In this site, we are going to give you some advice on some of the top things you should get when you are expecting a newborn baby or if you are a family planning on adopting a baby.

Even if you are just shopping for someone else baby or your newborn grandchild, I hope this site will be useful for you.

Baby necessities are the thing you should buy first, which are Nursery furniture, Home Babyproofing items, Diaper changing items, and Baby travel items.

Nursery Furniture

      1. Crib and CribMattress
      2. Bassinet
      3. Baby Monitor
      4. Rocking Chair
      5. Humidifier

Babyproofing Your Home

      1. Baby Gate
      2. Cabinet Latches
      3. Power Outlet Cover
      4. Furniture Anchors

Diaper Changing Item

      1. Have a least 2 packs of newborn diapers at all times.
      2. Non-scented Baby wipes.
      3. Waterproof changing table pad/cover.
      4. 2-3 changing table pad covers.
      5. Diaper pail or trash can with a lid.
